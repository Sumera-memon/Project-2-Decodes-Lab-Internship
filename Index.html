Why KNN Wins

The K-Nearest Neighbors algorithm works perfectly on Iris because the three species are naturally well-separated in 4D feature space — especially petal measurements. After StandardScaling, every feature contributes equally to the distance calculation and the Proximity Principle cleanly maps to botanical similarity. Setosa is trivially distinct (tiny petals), while Versicolor and Virginica cluster separately enough for KNN to discriminate with zero errors.
